About the Legend

☛ Vincent van Gogh, Vincent van Gogh, was born on March 30, 1853, in Zundert, Netherlands.

☛ He started painting at the age of 27.

☛ He attempted many other careers before declaring himself an artist.

☛ He created one of the most expensive paintings of history "Portrait of Dr.Gachet" which was sold for $82.5 millin in 1990.

☛ He cut his own ear. There are many theories surrounding the van Gogh ear-cutting accident. One of the popular thories is that van Gogh took a razor to his left ear and partially severed it after getting into a fight with felllow artist Paul Gauguin. He then wrapped the partially severed ear in paper and delivered it to a women at a brothel that both he and Gauguin used to frequent.

☛ His most work Starry Night was painted at an asylum at Saint-Remy-de-Province, France.

☛ Van Gogh only sold one painting while he was alive. The Red Vineyardwent for 400 francs in Belgium seven months before his death.

☛ From November 1881 to July 1890, van Gogh produced close to 900 paintings.

☛ On July 27, 1890 van Gogh shot himself in the chest and died 2 days after.
